Operating Rules

  • Primary metric: complete the daily ritual.
  • Target effort: RPE 6–7. Leave reps in reserve.
  • No maxes, no failed reps, no grinding.
  • Sharp pain, nerve pain, or worsening joint pain means modify immediately.
  • Wednesday is default recovery day, but it can float to another weekday.
